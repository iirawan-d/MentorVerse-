MentorVerse – Intelligent Academic Advising Powered by AI

Transforming student success through digital twins, predictive analytics, and personalized guidance.

MentorVerse is an AI-driven academic advising platform that helps students plan their academic journey while empowering advisors with real-time insights and data-driven recommendations.

The Challenge

Why MentorVerse?

Traditional academic advising often lacks personalization and timely support, leaving students without clear guidance. Despite the wealth of knowledge within our institutions, it is clear that there is gap between students and advisors.

MentorVerse solves this by using AI and digital twins to provide real-time insights, early risk detection, and personalized recommendations helping students and advisors stay on track and make better decisions.

revolutionize academic advising

MentorVerse Key Features

☆

Digital Twin

A real-time virtual model of each student that tracks academic progress and supports smarter decision-making.

◉

Predictive Analytics

AI models analyze student data to predict performance, identify risks, and provide early warnings.

✎

Personalized Recommendations

Students receive tailored academic advice based on their performance, goals, and learning patterns.

✦

Advisor Dashboard

Advisors can monitor students, track performance, and provide targeted guidance efficiently.

▣

Smart Alerts System

Real-time notifications for at-risk students with actionable recommendations.

◈

Academic Path Simulation

Simulate academic path to see the impact on GPA and academic progress.
