General Questions

1. What is MentorVerse?

MentorVerse is an AI-powered academic advising platform that helps students and advisors make better academic decisions using data-driven insights.

2. Who can use MentorVerse?

The platform is designed for university students and academic advisors.

3. What makes MentorVerse different from traditional advising?

It uses artificial intelligence and predictive analytics to provide personalized and proactive guidance instead of relying only on manual advising.

4. What is a digital twin in MentorVerse?

A digital twin is a virtual representation of a student that reflects their academic progress, and performance in real time.

5. Is MentorVerse available on mobile devices?

Currently, it is a web-based platform, but mobile support may be developed in the future.

6. What problem does MentorVerse solve?

It addresses the limitations of traditional advising by improving efficiency, personalization, and early risk detection.

7. Is MentorVerse only for one university?

The current version is designed for a specific university, but it can be expanded to support multiple institutions.

Features & Functionality

1. How does MentorVerse predict student performance?

It uses AI to analyze academic data and identify patterns that indicate future performance.

2. Can students simulate their academic plans?

Yes, students can test different course scenarios and see how they affect their GPA and progress.

3. What kind of recommendations does the system provide?

It provides personalized suggestions such as course selection, study improvements, and risk mitigation strategies.

4. How do advisors use MentorVerse?

Advisors access dashboards that show student performance, predictions, and allow them to give feedback and recommendations.

5. Does the system provide alerts?

Yes, it sends alerts when a student is at risk or when important changes occur in their academic status.

6. Can students interact with advisors through the platform?

Yes, advisors can add notes and recommendations that students can view.

7. Is the platform updated in real time?

Yes, the system updates student data continuously to provide accurate insights.
